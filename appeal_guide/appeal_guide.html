Step-By-Step Guide to Appealing Your Ticket

In Person:

You have the right to contest the issuance of a parking, compliance, automated traffic law enforcement (i.e. red light) or automated speed camera enforcement violation.

  • You have seven (7) days from the date on the ticket to contest.
  • If you do not pay on contest within seven (7) days, a second Notice of Violation is mailed allowing twenty-one (21) days to request a hearing. (28 days from ticket date)
  • You will then have twenty-one (21) days to contest the issuance of an automated traffic law enforcement (i.e. red light) or automated speed camera enforcement violation. (28 days from ticket date)
  • If there is no response after twenty-one (21) days, you will be found liable by default. You may petition to set aside this default judgment if you appear in person within twenty-one (21) days. (69 days from ticket date)
