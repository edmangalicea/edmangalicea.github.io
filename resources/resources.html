Important Links

To view the official information about parking violations, you can use the Chicago Parking Ticket Portal. You can use this link to contest tickets online, in-person, or by mail within Chicago. This portal also allows you to view the evidence of the violation, request a copy of your ticket, or acquire information about representation.

Alternatively if you want to contest your ticket and live in Cook County but not Chicago, you can appeal online at the Cook County Appeal Portal

Penalties for Unpaid Tickets

Penalties for Unpaid tickets punish repetitive behavior. The penalties vary depending on the original fine, vehicle type, and time spent not paying the ticket

  • Ticket Prices range from: $25.00-$150.00
  • After 25 days of an unpaid ticket the original fine amount doubles
  • If you have 3+ tickets, your car will be booted for 24 hours
    • If all your tickets are unpaid after 24 hours, the vehicle will be towed to an impound
  • Booting and impounding fees
    • $60.00-$400.00 boot fee
    • $150-$250.00 towing fee
    • $20-$60 per day your vehicle spends at the impound, for the first five (5) days
    • $35-$100 per day after, up to $1,000 in fines and fees
